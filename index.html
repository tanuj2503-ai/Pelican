Our Story

Founded on February 24, 2004, Pelican Confectioners Pvt. Ltd. began with a simple vision: to create high-quality, delicious confections that bring joy to every occasion. Our core activity involves the processing and preserving of fruit, vegetables, and edible nuts, which form the base of our unique recipes.

From a humble start, we've grown into a trusted name, known for our commitment to quality ingredients and innovative flavors. Our mission is to craft moments of happiness through our wide range of treats, maintaining the highest standards of production and customer satisfaction.
